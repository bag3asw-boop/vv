The Echo of Silence

by Elara Vance

2023 selection ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 | 1,294 reviews)
Philosophy History

In a world that never sleeps, Elara Vance explores the profound architecture of stillness and its impact on the human psyche. This seminal work redefines modern introspection through a series of lyrical essays that bridge ancient wisdom with contemporary neuroscience. Discover how the absence of sound becomes the ultimate canvas for self-discovery and creative evolution.

PAGE COUNT 342 pages
PUBLISHER Linen & Ink Press
PUBLISHED October 2023
ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Elara Vance

Elara Vance is a philosopher and neuro anthropologist whose work focuses on the intersection of ancient contemplative practices and modern cognitive science. Her previous works, including "The Architecture of Thought," have been translated into twelve languages.
